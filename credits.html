Credits

Flashcard Pixel Dungeon is the culmination of the efforts of many people. First came Pixel Dungeon by Watabou. Some time later, Evan Debenham forked Pixel Dungeon into Shattered Pixel Dungeon, improving it substantially. In 2019, I teamed up with some classmates to fork Shattered Pixel Dungeon into Flashcard Pixel Dungeon. My name is Johnathan Andersen, and the names of those classmates were:
